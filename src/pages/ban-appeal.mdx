# Ban Appeal

If you have been banned from Cordviews, you may use this form to appeal your ban.

:::warning
If you have been banned from Cordviews, you will be banned from both the Discord server and the bot. You may not use any commands, and **<a class="highlight">@Cordviews</a> will leave all servers you own**.
:::

It is highly recommended to use the link provided in your DM received from <a class="highlight">@Dyno</a> to ensure your ban is actually appealable.
